Dialogue Colouring

If you click on 'Colorize!', the text will be f-chat formatted for the dialogue!
Per Default, the effect is only applied onto dialogue between two " marks. This can be changed under 'Advanced Options'. Alt+Enter or Ctrl+Enter can be used instead of clicking the button!
